MyFSKPM App
Students at the Faculty of Cognitive Sciences and Human Development (FCSHD) face significant challenges accessing
essential academic information. Key information is often buried in lengthy, hard-to-navigate PDF guidebooks or on outdated
physical copies. This makes critical tasks, such as course registration and understanding faculty regulations, inefficient and
frustrating for students.
Problem
Main Objective
The project's primary objective was to design and develop a user-centered mobile application that streamlines student access
to faculty guidebooks and essential academic information. The goal was to replace the outdated methods with a solution that
provides quick, easy, and reliable access, thereby improving the user experience, enhancing accessibility , and aligning with
key usability goals.
Project Type
This was a group project for the KMK3323 Human Computer Interaction course. The project followed an iterative design
process, moving from requirements gathering to a paper-based prototype and culminating in a fully functional high-fidelity
Android application (APK)
Design Your Desire Interface
MyFSKPM App
The solution is MyFSKPM, a fully functional, high-fidelity smartphone application. The app consolidates all essential faculty
information into one accessible platform, featuring five key modules:
Course Plan: Provides full course descriptions, prerequisites, credit hours, and elective options.
Academic Calendar: Offers real-time access to important dates, registration deadlines, and exam schedules.
Contact Directory: A searchable directory for both lecturer and administrative staff contact details.
Faculty Regulations: A clear, well-structured section summarizing academic policies, grading criteria, and disciplinary
rules.
Virtual Assistant: An AI chatbot named "Mr FSKPM" integrated to answer student questions about the faculty
guidebook

Kampung 
Trombol App
Design Your Desire Interface
Problem
Kampung Trombol is a unique beach destination, but it lacks a modern, user-
friendly digital platform to promote its attractions, manage services, and engage
with visitors. An effective mobile presence is needed to streamline the visitor
experience and provide an administrative tool for managing the location's
offerings.
Main Objective
This project was a two-phase endeavor. The first objective was to design a
complete, high-fidelity UI skeleton for the "Trombol Paradise Beach" mobile
application using Figma. The second objective was to convert this UI prototype
into a fully functional, database-driven mobile application using the Flutter SDK
and Firebase backend.
Solution
The solution is a comprehensive, cross-platform mobile
application built with Flutter. The app serves two distinct user
types: Customers and Admins.
For Customers: The app provides a seamless interface to
browse and book offerings. Users can explore Activities ,
Services (like beach stays) , local Products , and Dining
options. It includes full user authentication (registration,
login, password recovery) , a multi-item booking cart , a
dummy payment/checkout process , and an AI-powered
chatbot ("Drake") for 24/7 customer support.
For Admins: After a separate admin login, administrators
gain access to a full content management system. They
have complete CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete)
capabilities over all app content, including adding new
activities, editing product prices, uploading images, and
deleting services. They can also manage user accounts.
Technology: The app uses Firebase for all backend services,
including Firebase Authentication for login , Firebase
Firestore as the NoSQL database , and Firebase Storage for
image uploads. The AI chatbot is powered by Google's
Gemini 2.0 Flash model.

Design Your Desire Interface
User Research& Needs
While the project did not involve direct user interviews, the
requirements were established through project briefs and
competitive analysis. The team analyzed other tourism-
focused applications, such as the Swahili Beach Resort app, to
identify essential features and user-friendly navigation
patterns.
Admin Needs
A secure, separate login.
The ability to add, edit, and delete all content (activities,
services, products, dining).
An interface for uploading corresponding images for all
content.
The ability to manage user details, including updating or
deleting accounts
Customer Needs
Standard user authentication (login, registration, forgot
password).
An intuitive homepage to browse all available offerings.
The ability to select packages, add them to a cart, and select
a date.
A simple checkout and payment (dummy) process.
A way to manage their own profile and change passwords.
Access to customer support.
